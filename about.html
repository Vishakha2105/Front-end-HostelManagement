Hostel

Residential campus with world class amenities & infrastructure, situated on the bank of Tapi River. Out station students getting admission at NMIMS Shirpur Campus must seek an admission to Hostel. Utmost care is taken to accommodate all students. The hostel has its own discipline and rules applicable on all resident students.
